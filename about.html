About Stray

Hi, I’m just a little cat... but I ended up in a huge forgotten cybercity far away from home.

Everything here feels strange: glowing signs, narrow alleys, curious robots, and dangers hiding in the shadows. I explore this world by climbing, jumping, sneaking around, and following my curiosity.

Luckily, I am not completely alone. With my tiny flying friend B-12, I can uncover secrets, solve problems, and search for the path that leads me back to my family.

This is my journey through a city that feels mysterious, lonely, and sometimes surprisingly warm.
