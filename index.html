A Cat Lost in a Forgotten City

Lost, alone, and far from home, a stray cat finds itself in a mysterious cybercity filled with neon lights and silent streets.

Without revealing too much, this world hides secrets waiting to be discovered. Strange robots wander the alleys, and something deeper lies beneath the surface.

Your journey is not just about escaping — it’s about understanding this forgotten place.
